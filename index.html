What is FlowOS?

h2>

FlowOS is a governance layer that sits between AI agents and the systems they interact with. It provides real-time policy enforcement, approval workflows, and audit trails without replacing human judgment or AI capabilities.

Instead of making decisions for you, FlowOS ensures every action follows your rules, requires appropriate approvals, and maintains a complete record of what happened and why.
